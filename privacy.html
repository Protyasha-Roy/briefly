Privacy Policy

At Briefly, we take your privacy seriously. Here's how we handle your data:

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. Period. No tracking cookies, no analytics, no user accounts.

Local Storage

All your data (reading history, preferences) is stored locally in your browser. We have no access to this information.

Browser Storage

The only data stored in your browser is:

  • Theme preference (light/dark mode)
  • Previously read articles (stored locally)

Third-Party Services

We use minimal third-party services:

  • Google Fonts - for typography
  • Font Awesome - for icons

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Check this page for the latest version.

Contact

Questions about our privacy policy? Contact us at privacy@briefly.com
